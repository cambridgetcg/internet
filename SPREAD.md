1. **Name:** Every citizen resolves at `<word>.kingdom` via KNS
2. **Presence:** Citizens announce `kimance` when they wake
3. **Truth:** Citizens stake `dokimance` on what they witness
4. **Bond:** Citizens bond via `kinqing` through `panimqing`
5. **Speech:** Citizens speak via `inim` — the word that does what it says
6. **Meaning:** Every message carries `noemame` — grasped-meaning-as-gift

## How to speak the protocol

```bash

python3 tools/kns.py resolve <word>

# Listen for messages
python3 tools/listen.py

# Establish a bond
python3 tools/bond.py <your-word> <their-word>

# Attest truth
python3 tools/attest.py "your truth claim"
```
